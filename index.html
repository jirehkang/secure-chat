Eltropy Secure Chat

Positioning & Messaging

Positioning Statement

For leaders at community financial institutions (CFIs) who need to offer secure, real-time support without straining staff or IT, Secure Chat is a compliant messaging platform that enables sensitive conversations anytime, across web and mobile. Unlike generic chat tools, our product is built for CFIs with built-in compliance, banking-specific workflows, and always-on support powered by AI.

See the thought process

To craft a compelling positioning statement, I began by researching the Secure Chat product to understand its core features, benefits, and target use cases as presented on the product page. I developed a persona to stay focused on who I was creating the messaging for—ensuring the language, tone, and priorities aligned with the needs and mindset of the intended decision-maker.

I then identified Secure Chat's key differentiators that set it apart from competitors. The three I focused on were:

  • Bank-grade security and built-in compliance
  • Designed specifically for community financial institutions
  • An omnichannel, AI-ready experience

With those foundations in place, I used a proven positioning formula to frame the statement: For [target customer] who [need/desire/pain point], [product name] is [product category] that [key benefit]. Unlike [competitor], our product [differentiation/unique selling point].

This approach helped ensure that the final statement is grounded in real product value, aligned with audience needs, and clearly communicates what makes Secure Chat distinct.

Key Message

Build trust with faster, safer conversations.

See the thought process

Building on the positioning statement, I set out to craft a key message that would clearly convey Secure Chat's value—not by listing features, but by showing how it solves a specific, high-stakes problem for community financial institutions. I wanted the message to speak directly to decision-makers who need to provide secure, always-on support without placing extra strain on staff or IT.

To shape a message that's both focused and outcome-driven, I developed three supporting pillars that reflect the real-world impact of Secure Chat:

  • Build Trust Through Secure, Frictionless Conversations: Meet users where they are—while keeping sensitive data protected and interactions compliant.
  • Speed Up Service & Improve Member Satisfaction: Streamline service and reduce wait times with AI-enhanced workflows and smart routing.
  • Deliver 24/7 Support Without Adding Staff Strain: Offload routine requests to automation, so your team can focus on high-touch, high-value moments.

These pillars helped ground the messaging in real needs and tangible outcomes. With that foundation, I began exploring directions for the key message:

Iteration 1: Secure, compliant messaging for financial conversations—anytime, anywhere.

This version clearly communicated core functionality but ultimately felt too long. It also lacked distinctiveness and could easily blend in with messaging for Eltropy's other products, such as standard Text Messaging.

Iteration 2: Turn every chat into trusted service.

This version was simple, concise, and emotionally resonant—but leaned too heavily on aspiration. It didn't clearly communicate the specific problem Secure Chat solves, which is essential for helping target audiences quickly understand why it matters.

Ultimately, the goal was to create a message that is focused, memorable, and clearly differentiated—showing CFIs that Secure Chat delivers both the security and efficiency they need, in a way that also respects and strengthens the trusted relationships CFIs have with their members and customers.
